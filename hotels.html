Jaipur, the pink city offers a number of attractions to tourists. The best time to visit this royal place is between the months of October to March, since the weather at that time is pleasurable due to ending monsoon showers and the winter season. This is the holiday season when this magnificent city of Rajasthan observes maximum tourists from India and abroad. Direct flights to major places also ply from the international airport here.
